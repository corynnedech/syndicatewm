Founded in 2004, SYNDICATE is a student-run and student-choreographed hip hop dance team at the College of William and Mary in Williamsburg, VA. SYNDICATE is a name formed from the acronym SNDCT, which originally stood for Student Network of Dance and Choreography Technique.

We hold auditions at the beginning of every semester and put on two showcases per year. SYNDICATE members come from all levels and backgrounds--no formal dance experience is necessary!
